FAQ

Quick answers to common StreamlyTV questions.

Do I need a VPN?
No. StreamlyTV does not require a VPN.
What internet speed do I need?
We recommend more than 50 Mbps for the best experience, especially for HD, FHD and 4K content.
Can I use multiple devices?
You can install it on many devices, but one active stream works at a time. Opening a second stream may stop or log out the first.
Can I use Chromecast or Apple TV?
Yes. StreamlyTV supports Chromecast and Apple TV using compatible IPTV apps.
Can I pause my service?
No. Once activated, the service runs until the expiry date and cannot be paused.
Does live TV have subtitles?
No. Live TV does not include subtitles. Some movies and series may include subtitles or dubbed audio, but not all.
Is there adult content?
No. StreamlyTV is family-friendly and does not provide adult channels, adult movies or adult series.
